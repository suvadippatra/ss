📖 Booklet Imposition

The Concept of Imposition

Automates Saddle-Stitching logic. Formats sequential PDFs to print correctly when folded down the spine.

Signature Terminology

  • 1-Fold (4 pages/sig): One physical sheet folded once yields 4 reading pages.
  • 2-Fold (8 pages/sig): Two sheets nested together before folding.
  • 4-Fold (16 pages/sig): Four nested sheets. Standard for thick booklets to prevent spine creep.
