📖 Booklet Imposition

The Concept of Imposition

Imposition is the architectural arrangement of printed product pages on the press sheet. This tool automates Saddle-Stitching logic, formatting sequential PDFs so they print correctly when folded down the spine.

Signature Terminology

  • 1-Fold (4 pages/sig): The simplest unit. One physical sheet folded once down the center yields 4 reading pages.
  • 2-Fold (8 pages/sig): Two sheets nested together before folding.
  • 4-Fold (16 pages/sig): Four nested sheets. The standard for dense academic booklets to prevent spine creep.
